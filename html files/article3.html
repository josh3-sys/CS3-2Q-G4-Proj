Causes of Climate Change

Most of the many things that we can do to prevent endangering our world even more is just as close as the screen that we face at this very moment, but not much can truly realize and accept things when not presented in a proper manner so take word on the list that will be mentioned below:

· Participate in many ECO activities like cleansing the amount of plastic that we consume and produce and produce items that can substitute items that may harm the environment.

· Assist on building and processing projects that can support the world to have less greenhouse gas emissions and increase the biodiversity of the life that is supported by the world.

· Lessen the use of limited resources in the earth like fossil fuels Minerals and more through new inventions, Recycling, and using vehicles that doesn’t consume fuel like bikes

· Choose independent leaders that can lead us to the right path so that we can assure success on our restoration in the future and that we can prevent even more ruination with dangerous projects that threaten the safety of our planet.

· Create and spread more biodiversity like plants and trees to lessen Carbon emissions that reside in the earth while also building more environments that other forms of life can safely live in.

· Grab your bike, hop on public transportation, or lace up your walking shoes instead of using vehicles that emit large amounts of pollution
